Control Bonds


Submission for GMTK Game Jam 2021
Me and my friend Daniel decided to take part in the GMTK Game Jam 2021.
It was our first time doing a Game Jam and the first one we did together.
The theme was "Joined Together" so our idea was that you are joined to your controls (WASD).
YOur controls is an object on screen that moves twice as fast as the player,
if a key goes off screen, you can't use that key to move.
Daniel designed 5 levels based on this idea and I coded it all
